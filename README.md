# How to Purchase Arc Raiders on the PS Store
This user guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to purchase Arc Raiders on your PlayStation 5 console via the PlayStation store.
## Prerequisites
* PS5 console connected to the internet
* Active PlayStation Network (PSN) account
* Payment method linked to your PSN account
## Steps to Purchase Grand Theft Auto on PS5
### Step 1: Turn on PS5 Console 
Turn on your PS5 console and make sure it's connected to the internet.
### Step 2: Access the PlayStation Store 
From the main menu, locate and select the PlayStation Store icon to open the store page.
### Step 3: Search for Arc Raiders
Head to the store's search bar, type "Arc Raiders," and press enter. You can find the search bar at the top-right side of the screen.
### Step 4: Enter Arc Raiders' Product Page
Click on the game icon to open the detailed product page. Here, you can review the product details, including game edition, price, release data, and system requirements.
### Step 5: Add the Game to Your Cart
1. At the bottom left side of the product page, click on the first button mentioning the game's price. You can also add Arc Raiders to your wishlist by clicking on the next button. 
2. Choose between the Standard Edition or the Deluxe Edition based on your preference. Once selected, the game will be added to your cart.
### Step 6: Choose Payment Method 
If you haven't added a payment method to your PSN, you can choose the first box to add your preferred debit/credit card details. Or if you have a PSN gift card, you can choose the second box to redeem it and add funds to your PSN wallet.
### Step 7: Enter Your Debit/Credit Card Details
1. To add debit/credit card details, either scan the QR code displayed on the right side of the screen. Or click on the "Use Controller Instead" button to enter your card details manually.
2. Input your card debit/credit card details. After that, input your residential address on the next page.
### Step 8: Confirm Your Order
Double-check your order and confirm purchase by clicking on the "Order & Pay" button.
### Step 9: Make the Payment
1. Once the payment is done, your purchase will be validated with a "Thank You" screen. Check your registered e-mail ID for a purchase receipt.
2. After purchase, you will be prompted to download the game. Choose to download immediately or download later from your game library.
### Step 10: Launch and Play
1. After installation, launch Arc Raiders either from your game library or PS5 home.
2. Enjoy your game.
